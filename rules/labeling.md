# Labeling Rules

## Purpose

Define conventions for figure labels, panel identifiers, legends, and text placement. Covers structure and formatting—not label content.

## Scope

**In scope:**

- Panel label format (e.g., A, B, C conventions)
- Legend structure and placement rules
- Label proximity and association rules
- Abbreviation and notation formatting placeholders
- Consistency requirements across figures in a manuscript

**Out of scope:**

- Scientific terms, gene names, or compound identifiers
- Journal-specific caption formats
- Typography tokens (see `styles/typography.md`)
