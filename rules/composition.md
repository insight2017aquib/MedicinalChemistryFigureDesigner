# Composition Rules

## Purpose

Define structural rules for how figure elements are arranged, balanced, and grouped to achieve publication-quality composition.

## Scope

**In scope:**

- Visual hierarchy requirements
- White space and density guidelines
- Panel balance and alignment rules
- Focal point and reading order conventions
- Consistency rules across multi-panel figures

**Out of scope:**

- Specific scientific diagram layouts
- Style tokens (see `styles/layout-grids.md`)
- Template structures (see `templates/`)
