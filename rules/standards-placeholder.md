# Standards Placeholder

## Purpose

Reserve a slot for user-supplied or maintainer-defined standards that apply to figure production. Prevents fabrication of journal or publisher guidelines within the skill.

## Scope

**In scope:**

- Structure for documenting external standards references
- Placeholder sections for user-provided requirements
- Instructions for importing journal-specific rules without embedding them here
- Versioning and attribution for externally sourced standards

**Out of scope:**

- Invented or fabricated journal guidelines
- Default scientific content standards
- Enforcement logic (see `validation/`)
