# Single-Panel Template

## Purpose

Define the layout structure for figures consisting of one primary panel with optional legend and annotation zones.

## Scope

**In scope:**

- Panel zone definitions
- Legend placement slot
- Annotation zone slots
- Aspect ratio presets (placeholder)
- Style and rule references for this template

**Out of scope:**

- Panel content or scientific imagery
- Multi-panel coordination
