# Comparison Layout Template

## Purpose

Define the layout structure for side-by-side or aligned comparisons between two or more visual elements.

## Scope

**In scope:**

- Comparison pair and group arrangements
- Alignment anchor points
- Divider or separator zone options
- Synchronized scale and proportion slots
- Style and rule references for this template

**Out of scope:**

- What is being compared
- Scientific interpretation of differences
