# Resolution and DPI

## Purpose

Define resolution, DPI, and dimension requirements for figure export across vector and raster formats.

## Scope

**In scope:**

- Minimum DPI placeholders for raster exports
- Maximum dimension limits
- Vector scalability requirements
- Print vs screen resolution profiles
- Upscaling and downscaling rules

**Out of scope:**

- Journal-specific dimension requirements
- Tool-specific export settings
- Color profile details (see `rules/export-formats.md`)
