# Specimen Notes

## Purpose

Provide a template for documenting individual figure specimens: design decisions, module references, and lessons learned—without scientific content.

## Scope

**In scope:**

- Specimen documentation template
- Fields for template, styles, rules, and validation used
- Revision history structure
- Maintainer notes and improvement suggestions

**Out of scope:**

- Scientific interpretation of the figure
- Fabricated design rationales tied to biology or chemistry
