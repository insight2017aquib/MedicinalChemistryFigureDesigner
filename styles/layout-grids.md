# Layout Grids

## Purpose

Define spatial organization systems for figures including margins, gutters, panel alignment, and aspect ratio conventions.

## Scope

**In scope:**

- Grid structure for single and multi-panel layouts
- Margin and padding conventions
- Panel spacing and alignment rules
- Aspect ratio presets (placeholder slots)
- Safe zones for labels and legends

**Out of scope:**

- Content placement for specific scientific diagrams
- Template-specific panel arrangements (see `templates/`)
