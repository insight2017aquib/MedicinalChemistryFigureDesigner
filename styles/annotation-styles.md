# Annotation Styles

## Purpose

Define visual treatment for arrows, callouts, brackets, highlights, scale bars, and other non-primary graphical annotations used in figures.

## Scope

**In scope:**

- Arrow and pointer styles
- Callout box and bracket conventions
- Highlight overlays and emphasis markers
- Scale bar and reference marker formatting (visual only)
- Line weights and dash patterns for annotations

**Out of scope:**

- What to annotate (scientific content)
- Annotation semantics or biological meaning
